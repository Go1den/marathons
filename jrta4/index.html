To heck with the pre-show. We knew the formula by this point. JRTA 4 was another milestone moment for the JRTA franchise. This was our first time raising money for four runners, and we also had our 10th unique runner from Japan attend Awesome Games Done Quick.

Highlights from JRTA 4:

  • Donkey Kong Country 3 three-player race
  • Sunday Night Alias (note: not held on Sunday)
  • JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure showdown between Claude and LCC
  • Dragon Quest III showdown between Darko and Pop
  • Yoshi’s Island race between Yamayuu and Poahr
  • Mega Man 2 race between Shoka and Zaraki
  • …and so much more!

In the end, we raised $7,726 to bring Yamayuu, Poahr, Shoka, and Zaraki to Awesome Games Done Quick 2018. Check out all of the JRTA 4 runs in this playlist.
